In 2020, the Yellow Jacket Flying Club passed a major milestone for collegiate flying clubs - we turned 75 years old!

The Yellow Jacket Flying Club cordially invites you to celebrate our 75th anniversary on Saturday April 2nd, 2022 at the Delta Flight Museum, featuring keynote speaker CDR Frank Weisser USN (Ret.), former Lead Solo Pilot and Operations Officer of the Blue Angels who will share some amazing videos of flying with the Blue Angels.
