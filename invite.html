The YJFC is turning 75!

In 2020, the Yellow Jacket Flying Club passed a major milestone for collegiate flying clubs - we turned 75 years old!

The Yellow Jacket Flying Club would like to cordially invite you to celebrate our 75th anniversary on Saturday April 2nd, 2022 at the Delta Flight Museum. In addition, we'll have a variety of fun activities planned throughout the weekend to bring club members past and present together.

Due to Covid, we chose to delay our celebration of this momentous accomplishment until we could safely gather with everyone who has helped over the years to create the club we know today. The time for celebration is finally upon us, so get your tickets via the link below!
