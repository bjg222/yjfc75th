The YJFC is turning 75!

"All my life, I’ve never been able to get enough airplanes."
--Astronaut Robert 'Hoot' Gibson

Please join the Yellow Jacket Flying Club as we commemorate 75 years of aviation at Georgia Tech. Since its inception in 1945, the YJFC has trained and inspired countless aviators and made invaluable contributions to flying history.
The 75th Anniversary Banquet will be held at the renowned Delta Flight Museum. What better way to catch up with old friends and make new ones than while standing next to a Douglas DC-3 with hors d'oeuvres and a drink from the open bar? Following the cocktail hour, enjoy a seated dinner, a YJFC retrospective, and remarks (including videos!) from special guest speaker, CDR Frank Weisser USN (Ret.), former Blue Angels Lead Solo Pilot and Operations Officer. After dinner, it's time to cut loose on the dance floor and show off your best moves before heading off to bed to dream about your next flight!
In addition to the banquet, the YJFC will mark this milestone with a weekend of events, including a welcome reception and an airport open house at Peachtree Dekalb Airport (PDK).
